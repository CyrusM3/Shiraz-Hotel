About Our Hotel

The History Of Our Hotel

Shiraz Hotel was founded by Alex Hamilton in 1950 when he purchased the Shiraz House motel adjacent to the Shiraz International Airport. Over the following decade, Alex Hamilton and his brother, Arnold, working together with other Hamilton family business interests, grew the company into a North American management and hotel ownership company, which became a public company in 1975.
